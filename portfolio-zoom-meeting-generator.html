Automation & Insights

Zoom Meeting Intelligence for Distributed Teams

Capture recordings, transcribe to Lithuanian, and surface AI-driven meeting insights and recommendations

Industry

Agency / Sales Consultancy

Scale

Multi-freelancer agency (distributed sales reps)

My Role

System Integrator & Automation Designer

Timeline

Q3 2025

Problem

What Was Broken

The agency used multiple freelancers with separate Zoom accounts. There was no central way to collect recordings, compare meeting quality, or track missed opportunities and individual performance.

Who Was Affected

Sales consultants, account managers, and the agency operations team who needed visibility into call outcomes and coaching opportunities.

Business Risk

Lack of visibility led to inconsistent sales quality, missed upsell opportunities, and poor coaching feedback loops, reducing revenue potential.

Analysis

Approach

We audited the agency's current meeting workflows, identified where recordings were stored (if at all), and mapped touchpoints where automated capture and transcription could enable analytics and coaching signals.

Key Discoveries

  • Many recordings were inaccessible for central review
  • Raw transcripts were in various languages and inconsistent formats
  • Sales opportunities could be detected by keyword patterns and conversational signals
  • Small process changes could unlock measurable revenue and efficiency gains

Solution

What Was Designed

Implemented an automated pipeline: collect Zoom cloud recordings, convert audio to text with Lithuanian transcription, enrich transcripts with AI to extract insights, scoring and action recommendations, and centralize results in a dashboard for managers and reps.

Automation Workflow

1
Recording Collection

Recordings are captured from Zoom cloud and pushed to a central storage bucket

2
Transcription

Audio transcribed to Lithuanian, normalized and time-stamped

3
AI Analysis

AI extracts action items, missed opportunities, sentiment, and sales signals

4
Insights & Feedback

Recommendations delivered to dashboard and via weekly reports for coaching

Why This Solution

Centralized recording + AI-driven analysis created a continuous feedback loop, letting managers coach reps, replicate successful patterns, and reduce time wasted on low-value calls.

Results

Better Feedback Loop

Managers received structured summaries and action items for each meeting enabling targeted coaching.

Revenue Improvement

+15% revenue attributable to improved follow-up and opportunity capture

Time Efficiency

30% reduction in time spent on meetings and manual post-call processing

Better Conversion

20% increase in closed deals from improved call quality and follow-up

Tools

Tools Used

  • Zoom - Zoom cloud recordings
  • ASR - Lithuanian transcription service (ASR)
  • OpenAI - OpenAI / LLM for insights extraction and recommendations
  • Airtable / DB - Central storage and dashboard (Airtable / DB)

Deliverables

  • Recording ingestion pipeline
  • Transcription + normalization module
  • Insight extraction and scoring model
  • Manager dashboard + weekly report generator
